What Are Atomic Habits?

Atomic = Small

  • Tiny changes
  • Marginal gains
  • 1% improvements

Habits = Compound

  • Regular practices
  • Automatic behaviors
  • System-based results

Why Small Changes Matter

  • Easy to start: Lower resistance to change
  • Sustainable: Less likely to burn out
  • Compound effect: Small gains multiply over time
  • Identity shift: "I am the type of person who..."
"Every action you take is a vote for the type of person you wish to become."
