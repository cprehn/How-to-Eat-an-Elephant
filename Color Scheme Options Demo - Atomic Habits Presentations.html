Comparison & Recommendations

Option 1: Progressive Evolution

Pros:
  • Natural progression feeling
  • Visually cohesive journey
  • Easy to remember sequence
  • Builds anticipation for next session
Considerations:
  • Less direct content connection
  • May not reinforce key concepts

Option 2: Four Laws Coding

Pros:
  • Strong conceptual connection
  • Reinforces learning through color
  • Easy to reference later
  • Professional and systematic
Considerations:
  • Session 1 stands apart
  • Requires explanation of color meaning

Option 3: Psychological Journey

Pros:
  • Emotionally engaging
  • Mirrors internal transformation
  • Unique and memorable
  • Tells a complete story
Considerations:
  • Light backgrounds need dark text
  • More complex to implement
  • May distract from content

Recommended Choice

Option 2: Four Laws Color Coding

Best balance of visual appeal, conceptual reinforcement, and practical implementation. Each color directly supports the learning objectives.
